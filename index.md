# Privacy Policy

**Kindly: Calm Kind Messenger**

*Last updated: February 26, 2026*

## Introduction

Kindly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and display name when you create an account
- **Profile Information**: Bio, profile photo, and intent tags you choose to share
- **Messages**: Text messages, voice messages, images, and videos you send through the app
- **Pulses**: Content you create and share as Pulses, including text, photos, and mood tags
- **Contacts**: Your contact list and friendship tiers within the app

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction timestamps
- **Warmth Score Data**: Activity-based scoring calculated from your interactions within the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the app
- Enable messaging and social features between users
- Calculate and display your Warmth Score
- Send notifications about messages, contact requests, and pulse reactions
- Ensure safety through content moderation and abuse prevention
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using **Supabase** (cloud database with row-level security)
- Messages are stored on secure servers and transmitted over encrypted connections
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Track your activity across other apps or websites

We may share your information only:
- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect the rights and safety of our users

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Update** your profile and account information
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Block** other users from contacting you
- **Control** your online status visibility and read receipt settings

## Data Retention

- Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
- When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed
- Blocked user records are maintained for your safety

## Children's Privacy

Kindly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Supabase** — Database and authentication ([Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Google Play Services** — App distribution ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
